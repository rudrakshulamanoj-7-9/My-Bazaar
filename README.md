## 🌱 Core Philosophy: Sustainability First
At its heart, **My-Bazaar** is built on the principles of a circular economy. The platform actively promotes sustainability by encouraging the trading, recycling, and reusing of products to reduce waste and support eco-friendly local commerce.

---

## 🌏 Problem Statement
Traditional local market buying and selling can be unorganized, making it difficult for:
* Sellers to showcase their complete inventory digitally.
* Buyers to discover local products quickly without visiting multiple shops.
* Both parties to track orders and pricing transparently.

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution
**My-Bazaar** is built as a direct marketplace that bridges this gap. The platform allows:
* **Sellers** to easily list, manage, and update their product listings.
* **Buyers** to explore nearby items, look at pricing, and buy directly.
* **Users** to experience a secure, responsive, and intuitive interface.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
* Enable direct and simple trade between buyers and sellers.
* **Promote sustainable consumption by facilitating the reuse and recycling of goods.**
* Provide an easy-to-use digital storefront for small businesses.
* Reduce overhead costs and dependency on third-party aggregators.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 👤 User Authentication
* Secure registration and login for both Buyers and Sellers.

### 🏪 Seller Dashboard
* Add, edit, and delete product listings.
* Highlight recycled or reusable items with special product tags.

### 🛍️ Buyer Experience
* Search and filter through local items.
* View clean checkout and product detail screens.
